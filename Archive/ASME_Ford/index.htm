Imagine the Following Situation

You recently hired into Ford Motor Company and are supporting an Advanced Program Team that is designing cars that will not reach production for another 3 years. The Chief Engineer for the Ford Edge is looking for innovative ideas to include in the program that will appeal to the target customer and make your product stand out in a very competitive marketplace.

 

Your Task

Form teams consisting of 3 to 5 people to develop an innovative idea to be implemented on the all-new Ford Edge.  Your team will develop an idea, capture the idea on a single page summary with up to 2 pages of illustrative drawings (if required), and present the idea to a panel of judges from Ford Motor Company on U of I’s campus on February 17.
